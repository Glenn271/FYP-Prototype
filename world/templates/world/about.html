About RentCommend

Learn about how we find the perfect property for you. Take a look at how to use our search engine, view property information, find local amenities and more.

Search Properties
Find Your Ideal Property

Use our search engine to find the ideal property for you. Enter your chosen area, the type of property/properties you would like, your maximum rent limit per month, and your ideal number of bedrooms and bathrooms. In the Criteria Priority section, our multi-criteria evaluation algorithm will filter your results based on your needs and provide you with your Best Match.

Find Properties

Best Match
Find Your Best Match

Based on your input on the Find Properties page, our algorithm will perform multi-criteria evaluation to analyse all properties in your chosen area. Your results will be displayed in order of how closely they meet your needs, with a Best Match displayed at the top. From here, you can view more details about the property, or add/remove the property from your Favourites list on your profile page.


Property Details
View Property Information

You can view information for individual properties from your results. Detailed information including location on a map, property images, a description of the listing, and a link for finding nearby amenities can be accessed.


Amenities
Finding Amenities

Using the Nearby Amenities section, you can view nearby amenities to a property in a given radius. From 500m to 5km, you can get an idea of locations related to entertainment, healthcare and education near the property you may consider enquiring about.
